60-Second New Player Guide

Join, get oriented, start a scene.

1. Connect

Use fivem.voxelbox.org:30120, then join Discord so staff can help if FiveM gives you trouble.

2. Open City OS

Run /guide, /passport, and /tour start to learn the city route.

3. Pick A Signal

Run /signal and choose a Story Contract that gives you a reason to meet another player.

4. Call A Scene

Use /crewcall when you want taxi, tow, mechanic, interview, tour, or business RP.

5. Watch The Pulse

Use /city and /events in game, or watch Discord for current city movement.

6. Claim Founder

Run /founder during Season 0 for a non-pay-to-win Founder Passport identity.
