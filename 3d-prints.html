Every custom print begins as a message — here's how it goes from idea to made.

01

Share the idea

Send the concept, inspiration, intended use, deadline, rough size, and the problem to solve.

02

Refine direction

We narrow size, vibe, finish, rights for submitted materials, and whether it's decorative, practical, or a gift.

03

Build from there

Once locked: pricing, payment timing, taxes, pickup or shipping, design prep, and getting it made well.

i

Custom order expectations. Made-to-order prints may have limited cancellation or refund options once design work, materials, or printing starts. If something arrives damaged or materially different from the confirmed scope, send photos so it can be reviewed. If you send characters, logos, models, or references, make sure you have the right to use them — VoxelBox may decline unsafe or infringing requests.
