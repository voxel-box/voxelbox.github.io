Server Features

What players can expect from Voxelbox worlds.

Every community server is looked after with the practical habits that help a free gaming space stay enjoyable.

Reliable performance

We aim for stable, smooth gameplay so community servers feel worth investing time into.

Low-friction joining

Clear server info, Discord guidance, and support channels help players get in without unnecessary confusion.

Active maintenance

Updates, restarts, fixes, and communication are handled with more structure than a typical hobby server.

DDoS-aware protection

Protective measures and careful management help reduce disruption when servers get busy or targeted.

Mod and plugin support

Where it fits the game, Voxelbox servers can support the extra features that make a community world more fun.

Backups and world care

Consistent save discipline and planning help protect player progress and long-running community projects.
