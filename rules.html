General

The rules everywhere.

1. Respect other members

Harassment, hate speech, targeted abuse, threats, doxxing, and repeated antagonistic behavior are not allowed. Keep disagreement constructive.

2. Keep it welcoming

Help maintain a friendly, community-first tone. Repeated toxicity, baiting, or disruptive attention-seeking may lead to removal.

3. Use channels as intended

Post support issues, feedback, LFG, and event chat in the right places so the community stays readable.

4. Protect the servers

Exploits, unauthorized automation, malicious behavior, or attempts to disrupt service or evade moderation are not permitted.

5. Respect privacy & safety

Don't share private info, leak staff discussions, or pressure others into revealing personal data. Report safety concerns promptly.

6. Creator conduct matters

Creators and community projects represent the broader tone. Professionalism, respect, and responsible promotion are expected.

7. Enforcement & appeals

Actions may include warnings, removal, restrictions, suspensions, or bans depending on severity. Appeals via support channels when appropriate.

General rules apply everywhere. Server rules add another layer per world. Temporary event/season rules count too.

Per world

Rules by server.

@minecraft

Protect builds, respect shared space.

  • No griefing, stealing, or bypassing protected areas.
  • Keep farms/redstone reasonable so they don't tank performance.
  • Ask before altering shared roads, hubs, or community projects.
  • Use mods and automation only if clearly allowed.
@palworld

Keep bases playable for everyone.

  • Don't block major routes, core resources, or spawn access.
  • Avoid excessive breeding/automation that creates lag.
  • Respect shared agreements on boss progression and resources.
  • Follow any posted team or guild setup.
@satisfactory

Build cleanly, communicate before big changes.

  • Don't rip up shared factories or train lines without checking in.
  • Label major production areas so others understand them.
  • Coordinate nuclear, power, and high-load projects.
  • Avoid abandoned half-finished builds in key areas.
@enshrouded

Respect progression and shared spaces.

  • Don't rush key progression without agreement.
  • Be careful with shared chests, gear, and stockpiles.
  • Keep base edits collaborative in public areas.
  • Treat boss attempts as a community activity.
@american_truck_sim

Drive for the convoy, not just yourself.

  • Listen to convoy hosts and route notes.
  • Don't intentionally ram, block, or troll drivers.
  • Show up with the right mods, DLC, and setup.
  • Communicate if you need to leave a convoy.
@beammp

Keep sessions fun and readable.

  • Don't intentionally ram, grief, or disrupt sessions.
  • Read the current session/event notes before joining.
  • Respect posted route, event, or host guidance.
  • Use support if your setup causes repeated issues.
@fivem

Roleplay in good faith.

  • Stay in character in RP zones; keep meta-gaming out of the city.
  • No RDM/VDM — violence needs a story reason.
  • Don't exploit scripts, dupe, or abuse game bugs.
  • Follow staff and posted city rules — they're the canon.
